About Sofi Shiti

Founded in Addis Ababa with a passion for Ethiopian fashion, Sofi Shiti has been creating beautiful, high-quality clothing since 2015. Our designs blend Ethiopian traditions with contemporary elegance.

We believe that Ethiopian fashion should be celebrated, accessible, and empowering. Each piece in our collection is designed with attention to traditional details, quality fabrics, and the modern Ethiopian woman in mind.

From traditional Habesha Kemis to contemporary fusion wear, Sofi Shiti offers versatile pieces that help you express your unique Ethiopian identity while feeling comfortable and confident.
