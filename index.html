About Sofi Shiti

Founded in Addis Ababa with a passion for celebrating Ethiopian culture through fashion, Sofi Shiti has been creating beautiful, high-quality traditional clothing since 2010.

We believe that Ethiopian fashion should be celebrated, accessible, and empowering. Each piece in our collection is designed with attention to traditional details, quality fabrics, and the modern Ethiopian woman in mind.

From traditional Habesha Kemis to contemporary fusion wear, Sofi Shiti offers versatile pieces that help you express your unique Ethiopian identity while feeling comfortable and confident.

Our designs incorporate traditional Ethiopian patterns, colors, and craftsmanship while adapting to modern styles and comfort requirements.

Our Ethiopian Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Sofi Shiti, rooted in Ethiopian culture

Community (መስተጋብር)

We support Ethiopian artisans and contribute to local community development.

Heritage (ቅድመ ታሪክ)

We honor and preserve traditional Ethiopian craftsmanship and designs.

Quality (ጥራት)

We use premium Ethiopian fabrics like cotton and handwoven materials.

Empowerment (ማጎልመስ)

We empower Ethiopian women through fashion and employment opportunities.
